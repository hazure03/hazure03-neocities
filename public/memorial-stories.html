One of my favorites is when me and two of my friends were having a sleepover. Two of us were in my bed and the third on the floor. Usually Luther liked to slither his way up so he'd be laying on my right side near the edge of the bed. He'd also usually have enough room to sleep comfortably there, except this time there was two people.

So, upon attempting to wiggle his way onto his usual spot, he just sort of fell off the bed onto the third friend on the floor. He was totally alright, after all he landed directly on top of them as they were trying to sleep.

They were not very happy that I was more concerned about poor Luther than them being woken up, lol
