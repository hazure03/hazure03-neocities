Project Boneyard

   Well, my bestie and I finished Project Boneyard's 'Locksock the Warden'. I thoroughly enjoyed it, I liked the new environment a lot, and new Coyle content is always welcome in my book! And it looks like I have my work cut out for me in terms of character analysis on the site, huh...

   However, it didn't feel exactly like the Coyle I know and love... It's hard to explain, but I feel as if Red Barrels really laid on the whole fanservice thing really thick. I felt this the moment I heard his opening line in the Project Boneyard teaser, and I was afraid they would go this direction. His new dialogue as a whole is very in-character for him, but I can't help that his character as a whole during this trial was very... fanfic-y? Similarly to his Valentine's Day poem in 2024 which is conveniently written out in the Misc section for your reading pleasure... but at least he references his cattle prod in that one. More on that later.

   I was afraid they would tone down his manic voltage-swinging side in favor of his sex appeal, and I feel like my fears came true. AGAIN, not to say I didn't enjoy what we got!! As someone who is VERY in love with him, I'm in hog heaven haha, and would not hesitate to be put in the Warden's shoes, but to me it's SO much more appealing when it comes from out of the blue. Just a little tease of his insane desires in the midst of a thrilling chase is what I'm used to... not him in heat for a whole trial.

   Leland has always has a sexual undertone, duh. It's one of his main themes (after all, sexual abuse in prisons is a very real issue and makes sense to make him perpertrate that. I'm sure it isn't his first time.). Making crude comments towards Reagents, sexually mutilating mannequins, touching himself during hallucinations, etc. To me, his kind of sexual has almost always been entertwined with extreme cruelty, and even his electricity, and while he was cruel to the Warden, it felt... not enough? Which feels crazy to say. As if they toned it down to make him less "scary" and more "sexy"...? His scary IS sexy!!

   I don't buy that when the Warden bites down on motherfucking Sergeant Leland Coyle's "Little Justice" [QUOTE]"I didn't hurt Little Justice, did I?" - The Warden, he just bitchslaps the Warden a little? And then looks around for the Reagents before yelling and then lightly poking the Warden with his stick and kinda kicking him??? He didn't turn that cattle prod on ONCE!!! HIS BATTERY WAS OFF!!!!!! He's never shown to use his cattle prod on the Warden at all! Or even on himself! What?? That's his whole THING, shoving his prod into Reagents, leaning his head back in ecstasy while torching his own inner thigh, threatening to make people "ride that lightning!!!"[QUOTE]"Somebody's gonna ride that lightning!" - Sgt. Coyle... he's an EXTREME sado-masochist, but now he feels like a generic 50 Shades of Grey-inspired alpha dom that occasionally makes funny comments [QUOTE]"It's a thin blue line and some big blue balls" - Sgt. Coyle. If the Warden bit him hard enough to make him bitch and moan the entire Trial [QUOTE]"You cause a good man this kind of lingering pain, you are asking to get burnt." - Sgt. Coyle, and is supposed to be Leland's fuck-pig, fucking fry him up like good old Southern BBQ!!!

   When my bestie and I were discussing his characterization post-Trial, I made a comparison that perfectly describes how I feel. Getting a whole ton of Coyle fan-service in one Trial is like getting a giant cake for dinner. I love cake!! I love sweets!! I will happily eat the cake. However, after eating a good chunk, I start to feel like it's lost it's luster. Isn't cake so much more special as a small dessert after a good hearty meal?

Phew!
