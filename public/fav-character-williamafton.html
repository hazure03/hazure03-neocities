Flutter

I'm not the best Dandy's World player, and to the disappointment of my team I always insist on playing Flutter. She's just the cutest ever, meta be damned!

She's a such a diva, and I love how the devs were able to give her so much personality despite her being silent. It's shown that the others can understand what she's saying (for the most part), despite her only speaking in "!" or "?" or even "..."
