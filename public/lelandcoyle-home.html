Origin

   A little bit ago, my best friend started playing The Outlast Trials (because one of their friends convinced them to play it). Their obsession (thank you, Franco Barbi) turned into mine pretty soon, after I was able to catch the game on sale on PS4 ($15 for deluxe ver. instead of $50???? YES!!!).

   I heavily enjoy horror media and I thought it would be a nice thing to get into together (I was just waiting for it to go on sale).
We started playing together as I learned how to properly play the game (debatable), and after a week or so I noticed this very special prime asset wiggle his way into my brain. Soon he was all I could think about.

Unfortunately.

Why

   I don't know. Genuienly I don't know what happened here. I adore how perfectly crafted of a villain he is, how fucking stupid he is, how he's such a well-written horrific reflection of the American police. He's so damn funny while still being so, so terrifying.
I have so many thoughts and feelings about him that I decided to pour it all out in a website and infodump into the aether.

   Anyways, feel free to take a look around and learn more about this awesome fucking character. I'll go back to looping him around obstacles and throwing bricks at his head.
