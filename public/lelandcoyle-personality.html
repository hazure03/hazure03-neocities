Traits

   Coyle is psychotic, paranoid, self-aggrandizing, and incredibly charismatic. He has a twisted view of justice, and seems to be aware, if just a little, of how dark his mind is [QUOTE]"Bitch needs to get out of my head, that is not a place anybody wants to be.".

   He also shows a fondness for alcohol and pistachios, as he was shown doing so before / during his interview with Clyde Perry, and from a voice line he can say while patrolling [QUOTE]"What I wouldn't give for a handful of pistachios.".

Electricity

   Leland has a deep facination (and fetish) for electricity. Almost all the ways he interacts with the player involves it in some way. His main weapon is his cattle prod, but he also references utilizing jumper-cables [QUOTE]"You have not lived 'till you seen a body dance on the end of set of jumper cables.", which can also be seen on some bodies in-game.

   During trials, his electrified baton and car battery emits a constant crackling sound, and can be seen with a blue glow in dark environments.

Justice & Paranoia

   Leland's paranoia and fears come from his insecurities and the thought of losing control. He's constantly rambling about a non-descript "they" (most likely referring to communists, Jewish people (as per his conspiracies) or other people he percieves as "threats"), convinced that these people will bring the downfall of America, and harm him the way he does to others [QUOTE]"...catch me sleeping. Some day. They catch me and they'll do with me every filthy and perverse thing their fevered native skulls can summon, leave me broken and ashamed and crying and they'll cut my throat and piss on me while I bleed out...".

   To combat this, he's dedicated to reign down a twisted sense of justice, torturing and mutilating "criminals" with his electric cattle prod. His idea of the law is whatever he says is true [QUOTE]"I said she's guilty so she's guilty. She is gonna dance on lightning."[QUOTE]"I am the law.", and has no issue in taking bribes, extortion, or claiming the innocent is guilty under no true pretences.

   As expected, Leland is incredibly prejudiced, and displays many racist, anti-semetic, misogynistic, and xenophobic views [QUOTE]"Reds and papists and coloreds and union goons and ornamentals and I don't know what country this is anymore."[QUOTE]"[...]Went all shiny and black.
